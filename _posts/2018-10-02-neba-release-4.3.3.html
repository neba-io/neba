NEBA 4.3.3 released

NEBA 4.3.3 Ships with Spring 4.3.19 and support for HTTP PATCH requests for Spring MVC. This is a maintenance release of the NEBA 4.3.x branch. User of NEBA 4 are encouraged to migrate to NEBA 5. NEBA 4 will be actively maintained until the end of 2019.

Highlights of this release

NEBA 4.3.2 Ships with Spring 4.3.19

Spring 4.3.19 is the recent most GA (generally available) version of the Spring framework, containing important improvements and bugfixes.
