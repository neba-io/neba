NEBA 5.1.2 released

NEBA 5.1.2 contains a critical fix for an issue introduced in NEBA 5.1.0. In NEBA 5.1.0, a Spring bean post processor was introduced that accidentally terminates the bean post-processing chain for non-model beans. Because of this issue, the @PostConstruct methods of Spring beans may no longer be invoked.

NEBA 5.1.2 also updates to Spring 5.2.2, containing important additions, bugfixes and improvements.
